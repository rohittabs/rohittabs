FAQ
Common questions
How do I receive my files after payment?+
Once you place your order and complete the UPI payment, your files will be sent to you via the same channel you used to order via WhatsApp or email.
How long does delivery take?+
I always try to deliver as soon as I receive your request. In case of a delay it just means I'm tied up at the moment, but rest assured your money is safe and delivery will be made at the earliest. You're welcome to follow up via WhatsApp or call anytime.
Are payments refundable?+
All sales are final. Since these are digital products, no refunds are offered once payment is made. Please check the sample before purchasing to make sure it suits your needs.
Which UPI apps are accepted?+
You can pay via any UPI app: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, or any other UPI-enabled app. A QR code will be shared with you after you place your order.
What is a Guitar Pro file?+
A Guitar Pro (.gp) file is an interactive tab file that you can open in Guitar Pro or the free TuxGuitar app. It lets you view the notation, slow down sections, and hear the arrangement. The file is password protected so you can view and play it but not edit the content.
Can I share the files with others?+
Files are for personal use only and may not be shared, redistributed, or resold. Each purchase is a single-user license.
