About

Hi, my name is Rohit Mishra. I am a music educator specializing in guitar, ukulele, and music theory. I create high quality tabs, structured music sheets, and backing tracks designed for clarity, timing, and real musical understanding.

These resources are built to support guitar teachers, students, and music schools who need reliable, well organized material for lessons, practice sessions, and classroom use. Every arrangement focuses on practical musicianship, consistency, and steady improvement, making it suitable for both teaching and personal practice.
