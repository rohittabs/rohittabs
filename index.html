FAQ
Common questions
01
How do I receive my files after payment?
Once you place your order and complete the UPI payment, your files will be sent to you via the same channel you used to order via WhatsApp or email.
02
How long does delivery take?
I always try to deliver as soon as I receive your request. In case of a delay your money is safe and delivery will be made at the earliest. Follow up via WhatsApp or call anytime.
03
Are payments refundable?
All sales are final. Since these are digital products, no refunds are offered once payment is made. Please check the sample before purchasing.
04
Which UPI apps are accepted?
You can pay via any UPI app: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, or any other UPI-enabled app. A QR code will be shared after you place your order.
05
What is a Guitar Pro file?
A .gp file opens in Guitar Pro or the free TuxGuitar app. It lets you view notation, slow down sections, and hear the arrangement. Password protected. View and play only, no editing.
06
Can I share the files with others?
Files are for personal use only and may not be shared, redistributed, or resold. Each purchase is a single-user license.
